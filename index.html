MY FAVOURITE RECIPE

KIMCHI

"Delicious Bites, Made with love"

Kimchi is a traditional Korean dish made mainly from fermented vegetables,most commonly napa cabbage and Korean radish, seasoned with a variety of spices and ingredients such as chili pepper, garlic, ginger, scallions, and salted seafood. It has a distinctive spicy, tangy, and umami flavor that comes from the process of lactic acid fermentation.

Main Ingredients of Kimchi

    Napa cabbage Korean Radish Chili Pepper Powder Garlic Ginger Scallions (Green onions / Spring onions) Salted Seafood Salt

1.Prepare the cabbage Cut the Napa cabbage into quarters, then chop into bite-sized pieces. Soak the pieces in salted water for about 2–3 hours, turning occasionally. Rinse with clean water and drain well.

2.Prepare the paste In a bowl, mix garlic, ginger, chili pepper powder, sugar, and fish sauce into a thick paste.

3. Add vegetables Cut the radish into thin matchstick pieces and chop scallions. Add them to the spice paste and mix well.

4. Combine with cabbage Put the drained cabbage into a large bowl. Add the spice mixture and use your hands (wear gloves) to coat each piece of cabbage thoroughly.

5. Ferment Pack the mixture tightly into a clean glass jar, pressing down so there are no air gaps. Leave the jar at room temperature for 1–2 days to start fermentation.

Then store in the refrigerator. Kimchi will continue to ferment and develop flavor.

RAMEN

Homemade Ramen Recipe

    Ingredients: 2 packs of ramen noodles (instant or fresh) 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth 2 garlic cloves (minced) 1 small piece of ginger (grated) 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional) 1 boiled egg (cut in half) ½ cup mushrooms (sliced) ½ cup corn (boiled) ½ cup spinach or bok choy Spring onions (chopped, for garnish) Chili flakes or hot sauce (optional, for spice).

Prepare the broth In a pot, heat sesame oil. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute. Pour in chicken/vegetable broth and soy sauce. Let it simmer for 5–7 minutes.

Cook the noodles In another pot, boil water and cook the ramen noodles as per packet instructions (2–3 minutes). Drain and set aside.

Add vegetables Add mushrooms, corn, and spinach/bok choy to the simmering broth. Cook for 2–3 minutes.

Assemble the ramen Place cooked noodles in a bowl. Pour hot broth with vegetables over the noodles.

MOCHI

MOCHI RECIPE

    Ingredients: 1 cup glutinous rice flour (also called mochiko) ¾ cup water ¼ cup sugar Cornstarch or potato starch (for dusting) Optional filling: red bean paste, chocolate, or ice cream

Mix the batter In a microwave-safe bowl, combine glutinous rice flour, water, and sugar. Stir until smooth (no lumps).

Cook the mochi Microwave for 1 minute, stir, then microwave again for 1–2 minutes until it becomes thick and sticky. (If using a steamer: steam the mixture for about 15 minutes until it turns translucent.)

Prepare the surface Sprinkle cornstarch or potato starch on a clean surface to prevent sticking.

Shape the mochi Transfer the hot mochi dough onto the starch-dusted surface. Let it cool slightly (but not fully, otherwise it will harden). Flatten and cut into small round pieces.

Add filling (optional) Place a spoonful of red bean paste, chocolate, or ice cream in the center. Pinch the edges together to seal and shape into a ball.

Serve Dust lightly with more starch and enjoy fresh!
